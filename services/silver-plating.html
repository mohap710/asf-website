Silver Plating:

Our Silver is bright to mirror-bright ductile deposits with excellent wear resistance. the deposits meet the requirements of QQ-S-365B, Type I and II, as well as AMS 2410, 2411 and 2412. This process is recommended for both electronic and decorative applications.

Features And Benfits:

  • Metallic brightener system.
  • Improved tarnish resistance over pure silver.
  • Excellent solderability.
  • Consistent high purity deposits.
  • Low porosity.
  • Recommended for electronic and decorative applications
