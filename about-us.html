About ASF

    Advanced Service Finishing (ASF) was incorporated in 2004 by founders who saw that inefficiency and low-quality work were standard industry practices.

    ASF's founders had a vision to create a company that would provide a high-quality, efficient and cost-effective service to the industry of the PCB, semiconductor and metal finishing.

    ASF is committed to modernizing our workflow and updating our employee training to meet these needs.

    We believe that it is vital to create an efficient, adaptable working environment so we can provide for our customers’ current needs and prepare for potential future services in the industry.
