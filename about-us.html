About Us
 
​Advanced Service Finishing (ASF) was incorporated in 2004 by founders
who saw that inefficiency and low-quality work were standard industry practices.

We dedicate ourselves to providing high quality, reliable finishing services to
our customers in the PCB, semiconductor and metal finishing industries, with
competitive prices and swift turnaround. 

We believe that it is vital to create an efficient, adaptable working
environment so we can provide for our customers’ current needs and
prepare for potential future services in the industry.

​ASF is committed to modernizing our workflow and updating
​our employee training to meet these needs.
